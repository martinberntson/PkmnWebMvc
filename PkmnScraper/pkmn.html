Eevee changes

  • In Generation 3, Eevee does not have the Adaptability ability.
  • In Generation 1, Eevee has a base Special stat of 65.
  • In Generations 1-4, Eevee has a base experience yield of 92.
  • In Generations 2-7, Eevee has a base Friendship value of 70.
